— The Method —

How to Decode

1
Each glyph in the cipher above stands for one letter of the alphabet. The same glyph always means the same letter — within today's puzzle.
2
Tap a glyph, then type or click a letter to assign it. Get it right and every instance of that glyph reveals at once.
3
You have 5 errors and 3 reveals per day. The Reveal button uncovers a random unsolved glyph for free.
4
The Frequency panel shows how often each glyph appears. Common glyphs are likely E, T, A, or O. Lean on it.
5
A new cipher arrives at midnight, in your local time. Tomorrow's glyphs will be entirely different from today's — no memorization carries over.
